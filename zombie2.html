SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: "2IC incident"(2nd Infected Contact)


ACCESS: OPEN


DATE: 8/23/23


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP's (NW Training Site garrison), 2nd T.F.C LEGION


EDITOR: IO Barry Lincoln

DESCRIPTION:

The "2IC incident" was a raid performed on the T.S.C(T.F.C at the time) NE training camp(NETC) by the "Infected Ones" hivemind. The raid would begin at the NETC, with Grade "5" "Infected" entity's posing as garrisoning MP's managing to scale the Southern wall(which was under construction at the time) and once inside, made contact with the 2nd Week 6 cadet platoon, who were on a routine morning 4 mile run. In a tragic turn of events, the entity's manage coerce the cadets into following them to the Southern wall, where a collection of Grade "1-2" "Infected entities were waiting. All 30 cadets were infected by the entities, bringing the IC presence within the site to 70. Following assimilation, a squad of MP's sent to search for the missing cadets, but upon their arrival to the cadet's last reported location, they were ambushed by Grade 1-2 "Infected" entities and also assimilated bringing the "Infected" presence to 84. The entities proceeded to make a mad sprint down "demolisher" trail with suspected hopes of reaching and assimilating all members of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, who themselves were performing a yearly inspection of the Site. With this revelation, A battle group consisting of the second T.F.C LEGION, and 4th MP Grievous company was deployed from D.O.M.A.I.N -2, equipped with lvl 2 biohazardous gear, 4 G.R.A.P.E.F.R.U.I.T, 11 C.H.R.I.S.T, and a squadron of N/X Strikers for support. Upon arrival and extraction of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L the Battlegroup proceeded to decimate the "Infected" entities, with only the Grade 5 entities managing to escape by posing as injured MP's and confiscating/escaping in an unmanned C.H.R.I.S.T after dismantling its GPS tag. This incident would bring the MP's competency into question after allowing for such a massive breach in site security, failure to prevent 30, week 6 cadets assimilation, and failure to contain the outbreak with aid from global T.F.C command. Fortunately, this incident does have its positive outcomes, as after much debate among the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, a bill pertaining to the formation of "clean-up" division under the MP's was signed.
