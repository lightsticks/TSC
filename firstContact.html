SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: “Operation: FIRST CONTACT”


DATE: 03/15/25


RELEVANT DIVISIONS: T.S.C. Military Police (“MP”) Division, 17th Military Police Command, [UNIT REDACTED]; Polaris Initiative Task Force, [UNIT REDACTED].


EDITOR: [REDACTED]


EDITOR’S NOTE: This article has had crucial unit and site information redacted due to the ongoing and classified nature of Operation: STARGAZER. To access the full version of the document, please consult the T.S.C. IT Department and Polaris Initiative staff for authorization as needed. Unauthorized access will result in the immediate quarantine of the user and/or the use of a memetic kill agent.

DESCRIPTION: Operation: FIRST CONTACT was an incident involving an unknown extraterrestrial object/lifeform that occurred during a routine patrol conducted by elements of the Military Police Division outside of the [REDACTED] location. While such objects have previously been observed by the T.S.C. and Polaris Initiative’s jointly administered Weddell Satellite Network (“WSN”) within our local stellar neighborhood, this incident marks our first (and mainly hostile) contact with elements of extraterrestrial life, albeit of unknown origin so far.

On the night of March 15, 2025, MP units attached to the [REDACTED] Company of the 17th Military Police Command responded to the sounds of a major explosion outside of Site [REDACTED] at approximately 22:15 hours, where their routine patrols normally took place. Footage and data recovered from Site [REDACTED] and the WSN’s satellite communications in the incident’s aftermath indicated that no objects had been detected by the base’s radar or infrared sensors prior to the explosion. This led T.S.C. Command to later surmise that the unknown object had been specially cloaked for a currently unknown purpose, though resulting events have led to speculation that the craft was likely for reconnaissance purposes. Although the initial unit detachment was sent to investigate the crash site, communications with the [REDACTED] unit were suddenly cut off after 12 minutes, leading Site Command to send another detachment of MPs (hereby known as [REDACTED 2]) to investigate the site and the [REDACTED] unit’s sudden disappearance. Upon the subsequent unit’s discovery of the crash site, audio-visual recordings recovered from their bodycams seemed to show a large crater, with a spacecraft of unknown design, presumably of non-human or terrestrial origin, positioned at the center of the depression. The site was surrounded by a large amount of burned and broken trees, with scorch marks visible in the dirt and plant remains from where the explosion had reached, indicating that the crash had been recent. While the unknown craft was mostly intact, parts of the heat shield and propulsion systems had been heavily damaged from an unknown source, though the burn marks suggested the damage came from some sort of energy weapon of unknown design. The more disturbing discovery, however, was the scorched remains of the first MP detachment discovered near the base of the craft, with the opened door indicating that whatever had exited the craft had most likely been responsible for their deaths.

While much of the remaining visual part of the bodycam footage has been partially corrupted by damage as a result of the ensuing incident, what is left of the audio footage indicated that a firefight broke out roughly 20 minutes after the [REDACTED 2] unit’s discovery of the crash site, with the sounds of gunfire and screams punctuating much of the footage afterwards. After around 30 minutes of continuous fighting in the area, the visual feed came back on again, showing what seemed like the remaining MP* engaging in hand to hand combat with the unknown, seemingly humanoid organism. The resulting fight lasted around five minutes before the remaining MP’s camera dropped to the ground, indicating that he was likely the last member of [REDACTED 2] to die. Shortly after, the visual feed would capture the face of the creature, seemingly humanoid with its face shifting to take the form of one of the deceased MPs, and recording their request to find the T.S.C. 's Administrator before the feed was finally cut out.

POST-INCIDENT:

In the aftermath of the incident, the Polaris Initiative sent elements of their Task Force to Site [REDACTED] to recover the unknown vehicle and analyze its components, while also providing joint security to the area. Although multiple attempts have been made to locate the creature responsible for the incident, no further sightings have been reported to date, leading to T.S.C. and Polaris Initiative to declare the quarantine of multiple sites within 200 miles of Site [REDACTED] until further notice.

*Addendum: T.S.C. MP Corporal [REDACTED] has been posthumously promoted to Sergeant and awarded with the Sirius Star for bravery in the line of his duty.*
