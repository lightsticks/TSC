SELECTED COUNCILMAN: FARCEUR

COUNCIL DESIGNATION: 2

CURRENT STATUS: off-duty

CURRENT LOCATION: D.O.M.A.I.N -2

DESCRIPTION:

FARCEUR, or "2", is the 2nd member of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L and just as every other member, has direct oversight over all T.S.C resources, and activities. Out of the 4 man squad , Farceur is generally considered to be the moral compass and brains of the group, taking the place of the First council member in his absence, and leading the group to develop efficient and "ethical" solutions to their problems. Farceur is also placed In Command of the T.S.C's SF(Special Forces), authorizing/overseeing the majority of their missions, and candidates, often being present at strategy meetings with both SF, Legionary, and MP command. Adding on to this, the T.S.C's weapons, GOI's(Groups of Interest), and scientific departments are all instructed to report directly to Farceur, with D.O.M.A.I.N 2 being the T.S.C's current sole research and manufacturing facility.
