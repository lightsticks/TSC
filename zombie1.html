SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: "BUSAN ARC" or "Z1 incident"


ACCESS: OPEN


DATE: 8/01/23


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP's (D.O.M.A.I.N -4 garrison), 4th T.F.C LEGION


EDITOR: IO Lowri Warner

DESCRIPTION:

The "BUSAN ARC" or "Z1 incident", was the T.S.C's(T.F.C at the time) first encounter with the "Infected Ones". A mass collection human like entity's led by a somewhat sentient hivemind puppeteering each individual entity. Contact with these anomalies was made at D.O.M.A.I.N -4, when one unaware legionary operative Luke Vandine began to mutate within the Sector four LEGION barracks. It is suspected that operative Vandine accidently contracted the "infected Ones" pathogen during his deployment in the Congo's and was unaware that he was carrying the Pathogen all the way up to his evac back to D.O.M.A.I.N -4. Regardless of how it "actually" happened, after his Mutation, operative VanDine proceeded to infect the entirety of the present legionary's, as they attempted to restrain him long enough for a pathogen related LST could be called in. D.O.M.A.I.N -4 would be placed on red alert with a major class on the Disruption scale, as the "Infected Ones began a mass rampage throughout the facility, dismantling many of the Domain's communication panels and infecting multiple staff and on-sight MP personal. Ultimately, the "infected Ones rampage would come to an end when two noble Elite class researchers Brent Nguyen and Paddy Vu managed to detonate the Domain's on site Nuclear Warhead, incinerating all "Infected Ones" entity's along with themselves. In the Aftermath, D.O.M.A.I.N -4 reconstruction would be placed under the supervision of the fourth C.O.U.N.C.I.L member, with the restructuring of the D.O.M.A.I.N security protocols being supervised by the third C.O.U.N.C.I.L member.
