SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: “T1 Incident”, "Terminator 1 Incident"


ACCESS: CLOSED


DATE: 2/11/24


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP’s (A.R.T.I.C. -Base Garrison), LEGIONARY ( 5th L.E.G.I.O.N)


EDITOR: IO Sarah Schwarzenegger


DESCRIPTION:

The “Terminator 1 Incident” or “T1 Incident” was the T.S.C.’s (T.F.C. at the time) first encounter with the entity dubbed the “Terminator”. The entity would be first spotted wandering aimlessly around the Myggbukta mountains by one of the T.S.C.'s NW satellites, after crossing into the Artic Base's 900 kilometer perimeter. A scouting squad of the 5th L.E.G.I.O.N was dispatched to apprehend the entity for questioning, as it was suspected that the entity was some sort of explorer, who trailed off from the designated village typical for foreign tourist. Upon arrival, the operatives found the individual standing semi-motionless in the 2 feet deep snow of the present blizzard, but as they approached the individual on foot, it suddenly began to violently shake almost as if it was attempting to imitate human "shivering". Undeterred the operatives continued to approach the individual, detaining it, and placing it back on their AEON class Gun-Ship. Mid-flight, the crew attempted to give the "individual" sustenance, believing that it was still an explorer who had wandered off from the village.

The "individual" remained silent for the entirety of the transport, being more inclined to staring at their feet rather than attempting to communicate with the rest of the crew. Once the Gun-Ship arrived at the base, the squad attempted to move the individual to the infirmary, as the hands of the individual seemed to be swollen. However, after taking 5 steps into the base's hanger, the "individual" would rip Operative Kolinsky's Mark-3 rifle from his hands, and began unloading its mag on to all of the present operatives. As, the flight crews ran for cover, Artic Command would initiate a full lockdown of the Eastern hanger in an attempt to isolate the individual for containment. But to the horror of everyone, the "individual" began to effortlessly tear through the S.E.C.T.I.O.N -7 blast doors, and proceeded to wander through the armory sector of the Base. Redeeming the threat posed by the entity as major, High Command issued a site-wide Lockdown, dispatching multiple MP squads in an attempt to contain the now "entity". While wandering through the bases armory section, the entity would replace the confiscated Mark-3 for the duel Venom-Pro semi-fully automatic pistols(which were still in their prototype phase).

Several MP squads managed to take the entity by surprise, emptying several mags in an attempt to "halt" the entity. Unfortunately, the MP's rifles proved ineffective in slowing the entity down, as it not only tanked all the expended rounds, but also cut down several MP's in the process. Ultimately, the remaining MP's devised a plan in which they could lure the entity back into hanger E, where one of the stationary K.I.R.B.O's could take a shot at the entity with a lowered 75 mm Demolisher round. The plan was heavily criticized by the Site's High Command, noting the lack of knowledge they possess on the entity, the possibility of the entity surviving the shot, and the possibility that the entity might hijack one of the stationary K.I.R.B.O's, leading to more damage of the site and personal. But as the entity continued to edge closer and closer to the hanger, High Command saw no other option but to approve the cruddy plan. Although most information pertaining to the events in the incident post "plan authorization" are under redaction from order of Global High Command. What can be confirmed is that the Entity is currently in the possession of the Polaris Initiative until further notice.
