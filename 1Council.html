The 1st council member or "1", is the head of the entire T.S.C council, with (previously stated)direct supervision over all T.S.C resources, they are also previed with information on the origin of each individual Council Member since the group's conception. Not much is currently known on the first council member, his absence from all T.S.C related activities being a common occurrence, with good portion of the T.S.C's D.O.M.A.I.N Directors have very little faith that the first Council member really exist. As of currently no word on the exact whereabouts of the first council member have been made. However, rumors spread by off-duty high command directors, pertained that the first council member was overseeing the construction of the T.S.C's first global D.O.M.A.I.N on the moon. These rumors however, have been quickly disproven by order of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, who themselves, came out with a response not long after, detailing how the first Council member was simply on a mental excursion throughout the New York Sector of the United States, particularly in the REDACTED regions.
