SELECTED DIVISION: "MP" (Military Police)


CURRENT STATUS: DEPLOYED


EDITOR: IO Eric Wang

DESCRIPTION:

The MP's, or Military Police, serves as the T.S.C's primary garrisoning body, with them providing security for all D.O.M.A.I.N's, supervising the Arctic Detention center and the transport of all its occupants, garrisoning T.S.C occupied providences, acting as security detail for high profile T.S.C officials, with their highest priority assets being the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, although, due to T.S incident S-101, information exposure between the C.O.U.N.C.I.L and the MP's is limited to High Command affiliated operatives. The division would be placed in the hands of the 4th Council member, or, "LEGATES", and despite their much more relaxed position, a typical MP is still a formidable opponent, even for that of a Legionary. This would be due to the fact that both groups partake in the same exact 15 week Basic Training course, only parting when the Division selection arrives. Along with typical Garrison duty, the MP's were also originally solely responsible for the cleanup of T.S.C related messes, and the search and rescue of T.S.C staff, POW's, or assets, but this policy would not last, as following the 2nd IC(Infected Contact) incident, a raid on the NE training camp by "Infected" entities, which saw the death of a large number of T.S.C operatives(most of which being week 6 cadets), a decision among the C.O.U.N.C.I.L decreed the formation of a new division, still held as under MP command, of course, but with much more autonomy. This new division would be made up of specially selected MP and LEGIONARY forces. Their prime goal would be to clean up any sense of T.S.C involvement at a particular location, along with enacting search and rescue missions in area's under T.S.C command that normal MP and Legionary operatives could not handle. This group would be given the title of RAC(Rescue and Conceal), and due to a limited number of qualified staff, they would be placed in the command of the 4th Council member, or, "LIGHT".
