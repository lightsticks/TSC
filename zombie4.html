SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: "MASS ARC" or "4IC Incident"


ACCESS: OPEN


DATE: 9/23/24


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP's (D.O.M.A.I.N -1 garrison, 4th Helicarrier Garrison, 2nd and 4th RAC platoons), LST Units A – 11/14, B –16/19)


EDITOR: IO Sam Jackson

DESCRIPTION:

The "4IC incident" or "MASS ARC" was the largest "Infected" raid ever performed on D.O.M.A.I.N -1, and is still considered to be the largest ever raid performed on a T.S.C sanctioned site. The initial raiders of the event, appeared to mainly consist of Grade 1-2 "Infected entities" with Grade 4-5 entities keeping their distance from the domain's R.A.G.E.F.I.R.E mounted armaments, and scouting out possible weaknesses in the domain's defenses. The estimated number of casualties from the first 3 hours of the raid account for approximately 4855 deceased Grade 1-2 "Infected", 8 Grade 4-5 entities, and 22 T.S.C operatives (the majority of which being MP's stationed outside the domain's wall at the beginning of the raid). After the 3 hour mark, the majority of the Grade 4-5 entities had retreated back into the vegetation surrounding the domain and the 2nd RAC platoon was instructed to bring all "Infected" carcasses from the battle back into the domain, either for incineration, or should their DNA match that of MIA operatives, be prepped for transportation to the arctic base for REDACTED.

However, upon the platoon reaching the battle sight, their AEON class Gun-Ship was hit in the rear fuel paneling, resulting in the compromised Gun-Ship exploding mid-air. Before the platoon even had time to react, several hooded humanoid entities emerged from the vegetation surrounding the domain, brandishing what appeared to be outdated T.S.C small arms (Nexus-Pro, Aeon-Pro, Modulus, Sling-Fire, Evolve-100, Siege-50, Raven-CS18, Hyper-Fire, and Rush-40's), these humanoids would procced to open fire on the RAC operatives, dashing to take approaching cover behind the corpses of the prior raid. Unfortunately, due to the RAC being formed as a non-combatant division, most RAC operatives weren't armed with T.S.C issued weaponry, resulting in several operatives being dispatched on the spot, with the ones possessing security side arms attempting to mount a defense using the craters created by the domain's 155mm Demolisher canons as cover. In an attempt to retrieve their pinned down operatives and to reestablish control of the sector, D.O.M.A.I.N -1's High Command sent out a request for Helicarrier –2(patrolling in the Capital sector) to be redirected towards D.O.M.A.I.N -1 to assist in the defence, and dispatching all still active garrisoning MP's to the Eastern wall, in an attempt to lay down suppressive fire long enough for the remaining RAC units to retreat back inside the Eastern gate, but upon the command being issued, several rust encrusted armored vehicles appeared from the vegetation, racing past the hostile entities and towards the Eastern wall.

At first, it was suspected that these vehicles were attempting to finish off the wounded RAC platoon, but upon closer inspection, revealing how the vehicles were strapped with an estimated 10 –14 pounds of C4, it was determined that the vehicles were attempting to breach the Eastern wall. With this revelation, domain high command ordered for the wall mounted armaments to fire upon the approaching vehicles in hopes of eliminating them before they could collide with the Eastern Wall.

Unfortunately, due to many of the armaments(especially the Demolisher cannons) being disabled for maintenance after the initial raid, the T.S.C was unable to prevent the vehicles from colliding and detonating, leaving a 2.4 X 3.6 meter gash in the P.L.Y.S.T.E.E.L encrusted wall. This breach appeared to be the main goal humanoid hostiles, as upon detonation of the vehicles, the humanoids would depart from their rotting meat shields and begin a "mad" sprint towards the exposed cavity, managing to take out an additional 12 RAC operatives and injuring 7 others in the process. Fortunately, despite the gash, the integrity of the wall managed sustain the weight of the arriving MP's who managed mount sniper positions atop many of the wall's "barrier" sections, eliminating multiple of the humanoid hostiles and forcing the remaining ones to retake cover behind the mass of "Infected" corpses. As on que, Helicarrier –2 would finally enter D.O.M.A.I.N-1 air space and proceeded to dispatch several AEON class Gun-Ships carrying LST Units A – 11/16, B –16/19 and a contingent of Helicarrier –2 MP's to the battle field.


POST-INCIDENT:

Following the raid, all of the surviving humanoid hostiles were detained and prepped for transport to the Artic Detention Center for trial and questioning. They would be held in Helicarrier -2's holding cells until arrival and LST units A –11/13 and B-16/17 were instructed to act as additional security personal for the transport. The 4th RAC platoon would take over the previous objective of the 2nd RAC platoon, taking over 38 hours to prep the site for repair, with the contingent of Helicarrier –2's MP's previously sent down remaining on site until D.O.M.A.I.N -1's security status returned to optimal levels. As for the T.S.C weaponry employed by the humanoids(the majority of which brandishing D.O.M.A.I.N -3 bar codes), they will be kept in storage at storage at D.O.M.A.I.N -2 until the site is fully repaired and ready to begin examination's on all of them weapons. The placement of the old weapons after examination is still in debate.
