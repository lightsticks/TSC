SELECTED DIVISION: "LEGION(LST)"


CURRENT STATUS: DEPLOYED


EDITOR: IO RICHARD Brick

DESCRIPTION:

The LEGIONARY, or "LEGION", is the T.S.C's primary, and largest combative Division, with almost every field mission that the organization has ever authorized, containing at least a platoon size amount of Legionaries. Unlike the other divisions, the LEGIONARY's are not under the command of a council member, rather, they are placed under the revised supervision of the T.S.C's D.O.M.A.I.N high command, which oversees all aspects of the LEGION, including deployment, sustenance, mortality rate, and moral. The purpose of this revised command placement will be explained shortly. Contrary to the belief of a good portion of the D.O.M.A.I.N staff, the LEGION doesn't currently follow a Roman Legion structure. Instead, the "LEGION" is broken up into multiple 10 person independent strike teams that either combine or separate depending on the mission objective. These strike teams are given the designation of LST or LEGIONARY STRIKE TEAM, with each strike team being designed for a specific role within the LEGIONARY as a whole. These could range from skirmishing combat, scouting operations, object/organism containment, naval operations, T.S frequency related incidents, infection related incidents, Intelligence gathering, etc.

Their designation system is very basic, with the purpose of an LST being placed on the letter that they are within and the specific "team" for that purpose is based on the number they are given. For example, say you need a skirmishing unit for a mission, you would simply select the letter that coordinates with "skirmishing", say A, and then within "A" you would see which number LST is ready for deployment, say –3, so now, the LST that you have acquired is LST UNIT A-3. This revised designation structure along with the revised abnormal command placement(as stated previously) being the direct result of T.S incident S-101. For context, prior to this incident, the T.F.C(3rd generation of the T.S.C) was still in use of the original Legionary structure, which incorporated aspects from both Modern Military formations, along with Roman Legionary structures during the rule of Emperor Trajan, along with this, command of the division was actually held by the entire original C.O.U.N.C.I.L, and although proving initially successful in the T.T.C(1st Generation) and T.F.C (2nd generation), The T.F.C (3rd generation) would be the era in which this formation sees it's end, as on the date of 4/17/24 at 3:31 pm EST, T.S insurgents would commence their first ever raid on T.S.C facilities.

This incident is given the designation of T.S incident S-101(as stated previously) and although records of the exact events pertaining to the incident are kept vague, from order of REDACTED, what can be confirmed, is that the incident has revealed the failure of the LEGIONARY's original structure, with the large battle formations being too slow in mobilizing to distant facilities/Sectors in Crisis, and the large confinement of operatives in one area, leaving them vulnerable to mass infection/corruption. However, the biggest outcome of the incident, and the T.S.C's main reason for transferring the division's command placement, would have to be corrupted LEGIONARY's from the incident revealing key T.S.C information to the insurgents upon their retreat, most notably, the exact location of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L. As stated previously, details pertaining to the exact incident are limited due to REDACTED from REDACTED, but what can be confirmed, is that the incident has resulted in a major breach of internal security among the overseers, and the decision for command placement of the LEGION under the D.O.M.A.I.N High Command as a precaution to conceal further public acknowledgement of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L's existence through compromised operatives.
