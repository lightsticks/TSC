SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: “T2 Incident”


ACCESS: CLOSED


DATE: 11/26/24


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP’s (D.O.M.A.I.N -1 Garrison, Squad -Delta), Special Forces (Squad -Hotel Sweepers)


EDITOR: IO James Connor

DESCRIPTION:

The “Terminator 2 Incident” or “T2 Incident” was the T.S.C.’s (T.F.C. at the time) second encounter with the entity dubbed the “Terminator”. This entity, as seen within the T1 Incident, possesses greatly heightened levels of strength, paired with seemingly infinite levels of endurance. All weapons used thus far have proven ineffective against this entity, with only 200 FPS+ rifles providing some means of slowing it down. Tasked with the escort of VIP Cayla Sircon, the lead researcher working on analyzing the T1 Incident, MP Squad Delta would be ambushed within D.O.M.A.I.N. 1's Eastern Cafeteria. Fortunately, the squad managed to direct the entities movements long enough for Sircon to be escorted towards her office in a desperate attempt to save all of her work before heading for the Northern blast room, as the premises were put on red alert. Despite Squad Delta’s resistance, the Terminator would manage to ruthlessly annihilate the entire Squad. Reinforcements in the form of Special Forces Squad Hotel would arrive shortly after, finding the door of the secure room bashed in. However, the entity was unconscious and Sircon was inside, shaking, but still alive. The entity was secured and put into confinement. The following was Sircon’s eyewitness account of what happened.

DESCRIPTION USER: "Cayla Sircon" “It was like the door was aluminum foil, I thought I was done for. I saw someone move behind the Terminator and put him in a chokehold, dragging him outside. It was as if the two were equal in strength, speed, everything. Eventually, the Terminator gave out and the ‘Hero’ disappeared before I could even say thank you. I absolutely will find a breakthrough in my research. I don’t want to see any other squad annihilated the way Squad Delta was.”

Following the incident, Researcher Sircon was admitted for 8-9 weeks of trauma-focused therapy at D.O.M.A.I.N -4, but per her request, her session period was shortened down to only 4-5 weeks, as stated by Sircon herself, "I need to get back to work". The body of the incapacitated "terminator" was transferred to the D.O.M.A.I.N -2 for examination, and dismantlement. Just as the previous model, this variant seemed to possess very basic internal security measures, making it all the more easy for the domain's researchers to gain access to the cyborg's internal security logs. In it, the researchers would observe the 'Hero' that researcher Sircon was alluding too, with the entity appearing to bear a remarkable resemblance to the previously deceased model, along with the present one. It is proposed that this 'Hero' is some sort of recommissioned "terminator", due in both part to the resemblance, along with the entity's enhanced durability when combating the deceased model. All research and assets of the 'terminator' would be transferred to the Polaris Initiative as per standard protocol upon encountering anomalous entities.
