SELECTED DIVISION: SF (SPECIAL FORCES)


CURRENT STATUS: DEPLOYED


EDITOR: IO Natalie Baker

The Special Forces or SF, is the T.S.C’s most prestigious division, with all operatives being specially screened from both the highest achieving MP’s and LST operatives. Under the command of Council member FARCEUR, the SF are typically assigned missions / roles that are deemed either too difficult or unwise for the typical LST or MP unit. This can include high difficulty raids, arrest, and containment, reclaiming of a site, hostage negotiations, or even personal security, most commonly for that of C.O.U.N.C.I.L members. The division itself contains two main battalions, broken off further into separate strike teams, similar to that of the LST’s. These two groups being the Commandos, and the SF themselves. While the SF are the best of the TSC human trained operatives, the Commandos present the pinnacle of T.S.C Bio-Engineering, with each operative being genetically enhanced with Compound T (courtesy of FARCEUR). A compound, which greatly enhances the individual’s active strength, speed, endurance, focal perception, cell regeneration, and abnormal immunity to MOST COMMON diseases and toxins. These commandos are typically assigned to CMP (Council Member Protection) and rescue missions with VERY little variation in assignment. Currently, the exact number of operatives serving in the SF is under REDACTMENT by High Command. However, it can be estimated that their total number is no more than that of a battle group.
