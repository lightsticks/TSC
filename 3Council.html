SELECTED COUNCILMAN: LEGATES

COUNCIL DESIGNATION: 3

CURRENT STATUS: off-duty

CURRENT LOCATION: D.O.M.A.I.N -3

DESCRIPTION:

LEGATES, or "3", is the 3rd member of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L and just as every other member, has direct oversight over all T.S.C resources, and activities. Despite being the most "recent" addition to the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, Legates has made quite a foot hold inside the organization, with him being one of the more "tactical" members, he has significantly increased security spending for each aspect of the organization ever since his recruitment, almost rivaling the expenditures of the Legionaries from last quarter. Although expensive, his investments have ensured that each of the T.S.C's current 4 D.O.M.A.I.N's are well equipped for any possible raids, infiltration, or GOI's based attacks. Accordantly, Legates is also currently in command of the T.S.C's MP legions, with him stationing at least a Company size group of MP's at each D.O.M.A.I.N, although, the amount of company's per D.O.M.A.I.N has been known to fluctuate in times of crisis.
