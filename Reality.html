DESCRIPTION:

This is a purely fictional organization that me and my cousins have pieced together over the course of the last few years. We're big Sci-fi and military nerds so we decided it be fun to create our own little militia organization. All of the information contained in this project is just pure make-believe and does not hold any basis in our actual world. It is literally just stuff that me and my cousins put together, as we thought it was funny or cool. There's no C.O.U.N.C.I.L, there's no combat with a hoard of zombies, the Polaris initiative is not an actual existing international body, and me and my cousins have no personal vendetta with Taylor Swift and her fans and the purpose behind the term of swifty is that it is how people(along with the fans themselves) refer to the Taylor Swift fanbase.
