SELECTED COUNCILMAN: LIGHT

COUNCIL DESIGNATION: 4

CURRENT STATUS: off-duty

CURRENT LOCATION: D.O.M.A.I.N -4

DESCRIPTION:

LIGHT, or "4", is the 4th member of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L and just as every other member, has direct oversight over all T.S.C resources, and activities. Ever since the original 3, LIGHT has been known to have been one of the more "radical" members of the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, calling for more drastic or even excessive actions to be taken, regardless if the situation in question calls for it. This "Radical" behavior has often resulted in heavy disagreements between LIGHT, and the rest of the C.O.U.C.I.L, with these disagreements usually ending with the C.O.U.N.C.I.L either vetoing all statements made by LIGHT, or(should the situation call for it), threatening to/enacting the P.U.S.H. -.U.P protocol. A special protocol, created by the 1st Council member to restrain LIGHT, should his behavior get out hand. Despite this, Light has been placed in command of the RAC(Rescue and Conceal) division, being the most common Council member deployed after a lengthy skirmish.
