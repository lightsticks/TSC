LAST UPDATED: 3/29/25

EDITOR: IO Shayla Ti

DESCRIPTION:

The C.O.U.N.C.I.L, are the main overseers of the entire T.S.C. They have unrestricted security access to all areas of our D.O.M.A.I.N.S, files, and safe houses. When present at any facility that is not D.O.M.A.I.N-1, their protection and well-being is the top priority for all elite-class staff members and up, with the protection of the entire council being placed in the hands of the MP's(Although recent discussion among high command has revealed that the council is possibly considering the formation of a group, separate from the MP's for their protection). The C.O.U.N.C.I.L consists of 4 members, the first three are of which the direct founders of the organization itself, forming the group on the date of REDACTED, slowly building the group's strength over time. The fourth and final member to join the C.O.U.N.C.I.L was T.S.C's first true operative, being recruited just in time for the initial zombie strike at D.O.M.A.I.N-4. They've been a part of the group for almost as long as the founders themselves, and although they didn't play a direct part in the group's formation, The C.O.U.N.C.I.L decided that their loyalty and bravery for the cause was enough for them be promoted as final T.S.C operative to joined the council. As of currently, all 4 council members are pre-occupied with other activities outside of group interest, and temporarily command has been handed over to the high command.
