SELECTED INCIDENT LOG: “Z3 Incident”


ACCESS: OPEN


DATE: 02/26/25


PERTAINING DIVISIONS: MP’s (D.O.M.A.I.N -2 Garrison, Recon Squad – Sierra), LST units B-17/24, 3rd RAC platoon.


EDITOR: IO George Cleff

DESCRIPTION:

The “Zombie 3 Incident” or “Z3 Incident” was the T.S.C.’s third encounter with the “Infected Ones” hivemind. At 16:36, D.O.M.A.I.N. -2’s perimeter was breached, prompting a red code for all security personnel within the facility. MPs rushed to their stations and carried out a desperate search for the intruder. The Surveillance Team spotted one cloaked individual, headed towards the domain's Intelligence Center. Though the individual did not seem to possess a firearm, they appeared to be masterfully skilled with a blade, with almost inhuman speed and prowess, slaughtering all security personal that attempted to detain them. Once they arrived at the intelligence center, the individual would pull out what appeared to be a cylinder pipe. However, upon closer inspection, several surveillance personal would note the "pipe's" eager similarity to one of Sci-Fi enthusiast George Lucas' 1977 creations in the form of the "lightsaber", particularly the hilt. These observations would prove ironic, as upon equipping the "pipe" and sliding what appears to be a built in switch, the "pipe" would ignite a 3 foot radiant rod, which is assumed to be powered by some form of concentrated plasma. The individual would proceed to effortlessly pierce and tear apart the reinforced blast doors before entering into the now vacant control center. Once inside, they started to leak data, focusing on files containing information on the “Z1” and “Z2” Incidents. Fortunately, MP Recon Squad Sierra would breach the now barricaded Intelligence Center, seemingly capturing the individual. However, with their back against the wall, the individual bit down on (what intelligence assumes) was a capsule in their mouth. Though this capsule was unique as it gave the individual "Undead"-like properties such as the "Infected "entities we have seen before. A major-class infection would begin to spread within the facility, with seemingly no way to stop it. Although Main Command intercepted the leaked intelligence and managed to retract the sensitive data before wide spread knowledge of the information was achieved, they were uncertain about the situation at the facility. Victor Ryler, a rookie of the Sierra Squad managed to broadcast one last message to Main Command so that they could respond accordingly to the situation.


TRASMISSION

USER: "Victor Ryler"

“This is Victor of Squad Sierra... the place is overrun and everyone but me is dead (clanging noises in the back). The zombies are trying to break down the door. I’m safe for only a little bit. Command... send a nuke. There’s no other way. And to the COUNCIL... be careful... there are people trying to recreate zombies though I’m sure you’ll find out eventually. Tell my family I love them." (the zombies break through the door and a single gunshot can be heard before the radio goes static).


POST-INCIDENT:

Following this disturbing transmission, LST units B-17/24 would be dispatched to retake the facility from "Infected" control and the 3rd RAC platoon would be dispatched shortly after to decontaminate the facility for eventual repairs. The now infected corpse of the mysterious intruder, along with his plasma-based saber would be collected by the RAC unit and transported to the Arctic base for examination. The intruder would be identified as one Richard Binsfeld, a 28 year old Caucasian male that was reported missing 8 months prior. Intelligence is still currently looking into the purpose of his disappearance and the possible connection it holds to the breach at D.O.M.A.I.N -2. As for the plasma-based saber, it was handed over into the authority of the Polaris Initiative after a mutual vote from the C.O.U.N.C.I.L, who deemed the Initiative as a more suitable group to contain such objects due to a "mishap" with Artic base researcher Luke Hamill.
